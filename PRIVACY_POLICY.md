# Privacy Policy for Kliply

**Last Updated: January 3, 2026**

## Overview

Kliply is a clipboard manager for macOS that helps you access your clipboard history. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your data.

## What We Collect

Kliply monitors your system clipboard to provide clipboard history functionality. This includes:
- Text copied to your clipboard
- Images copied to your clipboard
- URLs copied to your clipboard
- Files copied to your clipboard
- Other copyable content

## How We Store Your Data

**All clipboard data is stored in memory only.** 

- No clipboard data is written to disk
- No clipboard data is persisted between app sessions
- When you quit Kliply, all clipboard history is permanently deleted
- No clipboard data is ever transmitted over the network
- No clipboard data is shared with third parties

## Permissions Required

Kliply requires the following macOS permissions:

### Accessibility Permission
Required to register and detect the global hotkey (default: Cmd+Shift+V) that triggers the clipboard history popup.

### Clipboard Access
Required to monitor clipboard changes and display clipboard history. This is a standard system permission for clipboard manager applications.

## What We Don't Collect

- **No personal information** - We don't collect names, email addresses, or any identifying information
- **No usage analytics** - We don't track how you use the app
- **No crash reports** - We don't collect error or diagnostic data
- **No advertising data** - We don't use advertising networks or trackers
- **No cloud sync** - Your clipboard history never leaves your device

## Data Security

Since all data is stored only in your device's RAM and never written to disk or transmitted over the network, your clipboard data remains completely private and secure on your local machine.

## Children's Privacy

Kliply does not knowingly collect any information from anyone. The app is suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on our website with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store any personal data persistently, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your clipboard history through the app's "Clear History" function.
